About JP

My name is Jackson Porter, welcome to my online ePortfolio. This portfolio allows you to see my accomplishments, my work and my personality. Please feel free to contact me at any time.

Software Engineer
Technology has been a hobby since I could remember. I am curious; I want to know more! I am highly skilled with computers, with knowledge in Java, C#, MS SQL, BASH Scripting, MIPS architecture and basic Operating Systems knowledge for Windows, OS X (Mac) and Linux (specifically Ubuntu based distributions).I am currently taking classes towards my Bachelor's Degree in Software Engineering through Snow College. I have received two Associate's degree from Snow College in 2016 and 2017 in Arts and Science. View my highlighted schoolwork in the Software Engineering Page. I have worked as an IT Help Desk assistant at Snow College as well as operating my own support business in Sanpete County, Utah (http://valleyct.com).

Check out my GitHub or my Bitbucket for code and projects.
